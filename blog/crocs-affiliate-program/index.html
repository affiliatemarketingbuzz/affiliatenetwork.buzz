About Crocs

Crocs is a global footwear brand founded in 2002, originally known for its signature foam clogs. Over two decades, the brand has expanded far beyond that single product — today offering sandals, slides, sneakers, slip-on flats, weather-ready boots, and Jibbitz charm accessories that drive repeat purchases on their own.

What keeps Crocs relevant is a consistent pipeline of limited-edition collaborations, seasonal collections, and product innovations that generate ongoing consumer interest. The brand is not static. New launches create fresh content opportunities for affiliates throughout the year, rather than a single evergreen campaign with no reason to revisit.

Publishers offering affiliate marketing services across fashion and lifestyle consistently include Crocs in their top-performing campaigns because the brand's recognition reduces the persuasion gap — buyers arrive already familiar, and content needs only to guide the final decision rather than build awareness from scratch.
