How to Prepare for India's Peak Shopping Season

Most affiliates show up in October when the festive season is already loud and competition is at its peak. The ones who actually earn well during that window started months earlier — with content already indexed, ranked, and accumulating traffic before the season opened.

June and July — Build the Content

This is when you write what people will search for in three months. Best laptops under Rs. 50,000. Which HP laptop is right for college. How to pick a printer for a home office. Best HP monitors for work from home. These topics take time to rank — starting in June is the only way to be visible when October traffic arrives.

Do not wait for the festive season to start writing about festive purchases. By the time you publish in October, your competition has already been ranking for three months.

August — Back-to-School Buying Starts

Early festive researchers are already comparing options in August. Your June content is now gaining traction and catching buyers who are close to deciding. This is also the right time to update comparison tables, refresh cashback details, and add any new HP models that launched after your original post was written.

August is also when email lists and Telegram channels become more valuable — subscribers who joined during the quieter months are now in active research mode and receptive to curated deals and recommendations.

September — Onam Opens the Festive Window

Push the cashback offer through coupon and cashback pages. Activate your Telegram channel if you have one. Onam buyers in South India are high-intent, brand-aware, and ready to act on the right offer. This is also when you begin promoting more aggressively on social — short-form content showing specific HP deals performs well as purchase intent climbs.

October — Everything Pays Off

Navratri, Dussehra, Diwali — one after another. Push across every channel. The one rule to follow is specificity. "Rs. 12,000 off on this exact HP laptop model" will always outperform "big deals on HP." Specific offers with exact savings figures convert. Vague promotional language gets scrolled past.

If you have comparison content, update it with current deals. If you have review content, add a deal box at the top highlighting the active cashback. If you have a coupon page, make sure HP is featured prominently with the correct offer details.

November and December — Long Tail of the Season

Some buyers missed the Diwali sale. Others are spending year-end budgets or buying gifts for family. Corporate buyers are using Q4 procurement budgets. If your content is already live and ranking, it keeps bringing in conversions without additional effort — which is the real payoff of starting early.
